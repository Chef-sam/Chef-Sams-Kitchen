WHAT TO EXPECT

Our menu features a delightful fusion of classic and contemporary dishes crafted from the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients. Expect a diverse range of flavors, from hearty appetizers and crisp salads to succulent mains and decadent desserts. Our talented chefs are dedicated to delivering a culinary experience that tantalizes your taste buds and leaves you craving more.

Dine-In Menu

Starters

Roasted Beet Salad: A vibrant mix of roasted beets, goat cheese, candied walnuts, and arugula, drizzled with a balsamic reduction.

Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms: Large portobello mushrooms filled with a savory blend of quinoa, spinach, and feta cheese, topped with a balsamic glaze.

Carrot and Ginger Soup: A smooth and warming soup made with fresh carrots, ginger, and a touch of coconut milk.

Mains

Herb-Crusted Cauliflower Steak: A hearty cauliflower steak crusted with fresh herbs, served with garlic mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.

Vegetable Wellington: A medley of seasonal vegetables and mushrooms wrapped in flaky puff pastry, served with a rich tomato basil sauce.

Stuffed Bell Peppers: Bell peppers filled with a flavorful mix of rice, black beans, corn, and cheese, baked to perfection and served with a side of avocado salsa.

Desserts

Chocolate Lava Cake: A decadent chocolate cake with a molten center, served with vanilla bean ice cream and fresh berries.

Lemon Tart: A tangy lemon tart with a buttery crust, topped with a light meringue and a raspberry coulis.

Pistachio Panna Cotta: Creamy pistachio panna cotta with a hint of vanilla, served with honeycomb and caramelized figs.

Takeaway Menu

Starters

Classic Caesar Salad: Crisp romaine lettuce, shaved parmesan, croutons, and Caesar dressing (vegetarian version with no anchovies).

Tomato Basil Bruschetta: Fresh tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella on toasted baguette slices, drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Mains

Vegetarian Spaghetti Carbonara: Spaghetti in a creamy sauce with vegetarian bacon, parmesan, and a hint of black pepper.

Vegetarian Alfredo Pasta: Fettuccine in a rich Alfredo sauce with a medley of grilled vegetables and a side of garlic bread.

Vegetarian Pizza: A delicious pizza topped with bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and mozzarella cheese.

Desserts

Tiramisu: Traditional Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream (vegetarian version).

Cheesecake: Creamy cheesecake with a graham cracker crust, served with a berry compote.

Brownie Bites: Rich and fudgy brownie squares, perfect for a sweet treat on the go.
