RESTAURANT

Chef Sam's Kitchen is the flagship restaurant of acclaimed chef Sam, renowned for his culinary mastery and innovative cuisine. Nestled in a beautifully renovated farmhouse, Chef Sam's Kitchen offers a warm and intimate dining experience where guests can enjoy stunning views of the rolling countryside and picturesque gardens. In our open kitchen, you can watch our skilled chefs create exquisite dishes using the finest ingredients. Our menu features a generous tasting selection, showcasing locally sourced produce and herbs from our own garden. We take pride in foraging ingredients from the surrounding landscape, ensuring every dish reflects the rich flavors of the region. Join us for dinner from Wednesday to Saturday, or indulge in a delightful Saturday lunch. Our inviting and rustic ambiance perfectly complements the culinary journey, making each visit to Chef Sam's Kitchen a memorable dining experience. We strive to blend the charm of the countryside with exceptional fine dining, creating a welcoming atmosphere for all our guests.
